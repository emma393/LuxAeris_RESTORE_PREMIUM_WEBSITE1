Do you charge a service fee?

No. LuxAeris itself does not charge a service fee. If you decide to book, the only amount you pay is the ticket price and any provider-applied charges through the selected travel partner.

Do you provide quotes directly?

No. LuxAeris collects the inquiry and passes it to trusted travel partners and consolidators that may provide the actual quote options.
