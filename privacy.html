Privacy Policy

When a traveler submits a request through LuxAeris, the website may collect the traveler’s name, email address, phone number, trip details, dates, cabin preference, budget range, and additional notes.

This information is used to review inquiries and may be shared with trusted travel partners and consolidators that may provide quote options for the requested itinerary.

Website performance and user experience may be supported by cookies or similar technologies. Cookie preferences can be reviewed in the cookie policy and consent pop-up.

Reasonable steps may be taken to protect submitted information, but no website or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.
