Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-04

LuxAeris (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you may have.

Information we collect

  • Contact details you provide (such as name, email, phone/WhatsApp).
  • Trip request details you submit (origin, destination, dates, service type, budget range, preferences).
  • Basic technical information from your browser/device (e.g., approximate location, pages visited), depending on your hosting/analytics settings.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your request with curated premium travel options.
  • To communicate with you via the contact methods you provided.
  • To improve our services and website experience.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and to protect our users and business.

How submissions are processed

When you submit the request form, your information is sent to our form processor (Formspree) and delivered to us for follow-up. We use your information only to respond to your request and provide related service updates.

Sharing your information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information:

  • With trusted travel partners solely to provide requested options (only as needed for your request).
  • With service providers that help us operate the website (hosting, email, form processing).
  • If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

Data retention

We keep information as long as needed to respond to your request and for legitimate business purposes (for example, recordkeeping and customer support), unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your choices

  • You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us.
  • You can opt out of marketing messages at any time (we will still reply to service-related inquiries).

Cookies and analytics

LuxAeris may use cookies or analytics depending on hosting settings. If you enable analytics later, you should update this policy to name the provider (e.g., Google Analytics) and describe controls.

International visitors

If you are located outside the country where we operate, your information may be processed in another country.
